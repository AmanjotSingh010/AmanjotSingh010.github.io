FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does the AI replace my staff?

No — Resonant handles phone calls so your team can focus on delivering exceptional in-person service. Think of it as a dedicated phone receptionist that never takes a break.

How does calendar integration work?

We connect to your Google Calendar via OAuth during onboarding. The AI checks live availability in real time and creates bookings directly — no manual entry needed.

Can the AI speak multiple languages?

Yes. Resonant automatically detects the caller's language and responds accordingly. We currently support English, German, French, Spanish, and Italian — with more being added.

What if the AI can't handle a call?

The AI is trained to handle the vast majority of calls. For complex edge cases, it can gracefully transfer the call to a staff member or offer to take a message.

Do we need to change our phone number?

Not at all. We use SIP forwarding to connect Resonant to your existing landline. Your restaurant number stays exactly the same — customers won't notice any difference.

How long does setup take?

Most restaurants are fully live within 48 hours of our onboarding call. We handle all the configuration — you just need to provide your menu, hours, and calendar access.

Is my customer data safe?

Absolutely. We're fully GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted, stored securely in the EU, and customers can request deletion at any time. We never sell data to third parties.

Can it handle takeaway orders?

Yes — the AI collects full takeaway orders including items, quantities, and timing. Orders are stored in your dashboard and can be forwarded to your kitchen or POS system.
